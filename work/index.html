Work

For the last years, I've been leading and contributing in different sides of mobile consumer apps. I'm currently the Engineering Manager at Chopra.

Previously, I worked at a number of different retail companies in NYC, always around mobile development, including:

  • Moda Operandi
  • Hudson's Bay Company, starting as a Lead Engineer (a hands-on Engineer Manager position) at the GILT team, then a platform team and finally doing backend development for the Mobile Middleware Team in Scala.
  • JackThreads and his parent company Thrillist before they split up. On both Thrillist and JackThreads native apps.

I've also freelanced and advised a few companies in NYC, including Equip, where I helped with their backend, infrastructure, mobile development and off-shore management needs.

During my time in Silicon Valley I worked at Eversnap, a photo and video sharing startup, as Lead iOS Developer.

Previously I worked as iOS Developer for 2 years in Golden Gekko, an award-winning international mobile agency acquired by DMI based in Phnom Penh, Cambodia.

Before Golden Gekko, I was part of a J2EE development team building an ERP and led a small mobile project on Compact.NET for a subsidiary company of IKEA in Canary Islands for 2 years. I also worked as Functional Consultant in the Health Industry with Siemens Healthcare moving around Canary Islands and Madrid region. I also worked in Paris, France one year as .NET Full Stack Developer in Paris for B3G Telecom.
